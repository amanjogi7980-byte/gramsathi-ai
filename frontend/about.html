🌾 About GramSathi AI


GramSathi AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help rural citizens access government schemes, documents, complaints services and digital assistance from a single platform.


Our mission is to bridge the gap between rural communities and digital government services using Artificial Intelligence.


Features:


✅ Government Scheme Finder

✅ Document Checker

✅ AI Assistant Chatbot

✅ Village Complaint Portal


Technology Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask and Gemini AI.
